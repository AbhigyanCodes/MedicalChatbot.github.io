MALARIA

Malaria is a mosquito-borne infectious disease that affects humans and other animals. It is caused by parasitic protozoans belonging to the genus Plasmodium.

Symptoms:

Fever

Chills

Sweating

Headaches

Nausea and vomiting

Cures and Prevention:

There are effective antimalarial drugs available for treatment, and various preventive measures include the use of bed nets, insect repellents, and antimalarial medications.
