Common Cold

The common cold is a viral infection of the upper respiratory tract. It is characterized by symptoms such as a runny or stuffy nose, sneezing, coughing, and sometimes fever.

Symptoms

  • Runny or stuffy nose
  • Sneezing
  • Coughing
  • Sore throat
  • Fatigue

Prevention and Management

Preventing the common cold involves good hygiene practices, such as frequent handwashing and avoiding close contact with infected individuals. Management includes rest, staying hydrated, and over-the-counter medications to relieve symptoms.
