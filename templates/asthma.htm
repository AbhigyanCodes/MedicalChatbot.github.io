Asthma

Asthma is a chronic respiratory condition characterized by inflammation of the airways, leading to difficulty in breathing.

Symptoms

  • Shortness of breath
  • Coughing
  • Wheezing
  • Chest tightness
  • Difficulty in breathing

Prevention and Cure

Asthma can be managed with medications, lifestyle changes, and avoiding triggers. Regular check-ups with a healthcare professional are essential for effective management.
