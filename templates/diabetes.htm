Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body turns food into energy. It results in high blood sugar levels.

Symptoms

  • Frequent urination
  • Excessive thirst
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fatigue
  • Blurred vision

Prevention and Management

Managing diabetes involves a healthy diet, regular exercise, and medications as prescribed by a healthcare professional. Monitoring blood sugar levels is crucial for effective management.
