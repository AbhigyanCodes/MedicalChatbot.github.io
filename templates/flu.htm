Flu

Influenza, commonly known as the flu, is a contagious respiratory illness caused by influenza viruses. It can range from mild to severe and can lead to complications.

Symptoms

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Sore throat
  • Body aches
  • Fatigue

Prevention and Management

Preventing the flu involves vaccination, practicing good hygiene, and avoiding close contact with infected individuals. Management includes rest, hydration, and antiviral medications as prescribed by a healthcare professional.
