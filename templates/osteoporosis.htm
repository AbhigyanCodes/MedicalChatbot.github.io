Osteoporosis

Osteoporosis is a condition characterized by weakened bones that are more susceptible to fractures. It occurs when the density and quality of bone are reduced.

Symptoms

  • Back pain
  • Loss of height over time
  • Stooped posture
  • Frequent bone fractures, especially in the wrist, hip, or spine
  • Decreased grip strength

Prevention and Management

Preventing osteoporosis involves a diet rich in calcium and vitamin D, regular weight-bearing exercise, and avoiding smoking and excessive alcohol consumption. Management may include medications and lifestyle modifications as recommended by a healthcare professional.
